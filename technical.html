What is an If Power Apps?
Allows users to build custom business applications for various platforms (web, mobile).

            Power Apps is a tool that allows you to create custom apps, leveraging many of the features of the Microsoft 365 and the Power Platform.
        
            Apps can be accessed via mobile devices or via the browser, with possibilities for integration with different Microsoft 365 tools such as Teams and SharePoint.
What is an Power Automate?
Enables users to automate repetitive tasks and workflows across different applications and services. 

            Power Automate allows you to optimize your business processes across your organization and automate repetitive tasks.

            This service helps you create automated workflows between your favorite apps and services to synchronize files, get notifications, collect data, and more.
What is a Power BI?
Provides data visualization and business intelligence capabilities, allowing users to analyze data and gain insights. 

            Power BI is a collection of software services, apps, and connectors that work together to turn your unrelated sources of data into coherent, visually immersive, and interactive insights. 

            Your data might be an Excel spreadsheet, or a collection of cloud-based and on-premises hybrid data warehouses.
What is a Power Pages?
Provides data visualization and business intelligence capabilities, allowing users to analyze data and gain insights.
